Bemnet Yekoyesew Worku


Hi! I am the developer of this website and the founder of GoCareering. I am 15 years old and a 10th grade student at Green Hills Academy. I started GoCareering for my personal project assignment that I have been working on since grade 9. I got the idea when I was discussing with my father what I wanted to be in the future and I hadn't made my decision yet and neither did I have much information about the variety of the careers. So, we got the idea of carrying out different interviews with different career specialists and recording them so that we can also provide it to other highschool students who are in my situation. I started to contact the people I knew and interviewed them and made my interviews public as planned. I really hope you gain as much information as possible and I wish you a happy careering!

GoCareering


GoCareering is a career advisory website and podcast aimed at aiding high school students to have plenty of information about the different careers available so they can make well informed career choices. It provides advice straight from the mouth of an expert making it a trusted and reliable source of information. It helps students to be able to answer the question, "What would you like to be in the future?" without hesitation by providing adequate information making the decision easier for them as they can evaluate the positive and negative sides to the careers.
